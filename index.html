Other Projects——

Projects I participated in during my study

HMI Design for Autonomous Driving Mode Switching

This project designs the transition from Manual to Autonomous Driving mode in self-driving cars. It includes creating interactive elements for a seamless and intuitive handover from driver to vehicle control.

Fire Protection System Design for Urban Villages

Urban villages in China face fire risks due to dense housing and narrow roads. This project uses smart city tech to improve fire response and support.

"Place Life" Series Stationery Product Design

This project integrates the Four Treasures of the Study with traditional Chinese mortise and tenon joints and garden design to promote a slow-paced lifestyle.

Gobino Company systemic design

Using system design methodology, develop a strategic plan for Guido Gobino chocolate company. Research the supply chain, propose cocoa bean shell reuse strategies, and enhance digital media promotion to achieve sustainability.
